Eat Healthy/Stay in shape

Eating healthy and staying in shape can easily be a bonus to your hockey
skills, and make you so much better, just by being in shape. A good
example of this is how every car needs gas to run, and just like those cars,
our bodies need the best fuel to last longer on the ice, skate faster, and
dominate. We need a lot of nutrients in our bodies, so it is important to
always eat our fruits, the proper foods so that we play better on the ice.
Also, staying in shape is very important when it comes to playing hockey as
well, because it makes it very hard to stop you, which is extremely important.

Because our bodies need a lot of nutrients, you need to be eating healthy
foods like fruits, vegetables, our proteins (meat) and our carbs (rice,
potatoes, etc.), and lots of water. Those foods and drinks will give you
exactly the energy you need to be performing highly on the ice. Now,
staying in shape, I will talk about more on “page 5”, but I will still
address some key parts. Staying in shape is really important for hockey.
Staying in shape means you have to be working out, off ice, not
necessarily in a gym, but you can do many exercises anywhere just with
your body. Being in shape and strong will benefit you a lot, meaning you
will take hits and feel nothing, it'll be hard for people to take the puck
off of you, and it'll make you have better endurance, and have quicker
recoveries on the ice. A healthy diet and good programs to stay in shape,
is very important to improving your game in hockey.
